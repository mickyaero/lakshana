SERVICE BULLETIN

Service Bulletin No: SB-BOE-71-145
Issue Date:          07/03/2026
Revision Status:     Revision 02
ATA Chapter:         71 — Power Plant
Classification:      Recommended

SUBJECT

Engine Fuel Pump Inspection

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Boeing 737 MAX 8
Serial Numbers:      MSN 3130 through MSN 3731
Engine(s):           CFM LEAP-1B27
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-BOE-71-472 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address potential premature wear of impeller bearing under specific operating conditions. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Inspect impeller bearing per Figure 2 using borescope. If wear exceeds 0.15 mm, replace per AMM 73-12-04.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 6,000 flight hours
or 6 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            2 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 1,966
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
