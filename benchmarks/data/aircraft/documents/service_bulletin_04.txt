SERVICE BULLETIN

Service Bulletin No: SB-AIR-36-689
Issue Date:          14/06/2026
Revision Status:     Original Issue
ATA Chapter:         36 — Pneumatic
Classification:      Mandatory

SUBJECT

Main Landing Gear Pintle Pin Inspection

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Airbus A320neo
Serial Numbers:      MSN 3025 through MSN 3484
Engine(s):           PW1127G-JM
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-AIR-36-251 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address small population of pintle pins found with surface micro-cracks during teardown. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Perform fluorescent dye penetrant inspection per NDT-32-08-PT-04 on next A-Check.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 1,500 flight hours
or 6 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            14 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 3,122
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
