SUBJECT

APU Air Inlet Door Hinge Replacement

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Airbus A321neo
Serial Numbers:      MSN 1264 through MSN 1738
Engine(s):           CFM LEAP-1A26
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-AIR-27-438 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address hinge bushing wear leading to door rattle on ground operations. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

BACKGROUND AND RISK ASSESSMENT

Continued surveillance of the in-service fleet has revealed that approximately 4% of the affected population exhibits a measurable performance shift in the subject system after 6,000 flight hours. The shift is gradual and does not produce any flight deck indication, but can lead to a small reduction in system efficiency. Compliance with this Service Bulletin will restore performance to within the original specification and is recommended at the next scheduled maintenance opportunity.

Risk classification per FHA: MAJOR — operator action required.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Replace bushing P/N APU-INLT-BSH-04 with improved CRES bushing P/N APU-INLT-BSH-05.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 6,000 flight hours
or 6 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            8 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 3,073
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
