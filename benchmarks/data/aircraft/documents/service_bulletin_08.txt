Revision Status:     Revision 02
ATA Chapter:         21 — Air Conditioning
Classification:      Recommended

SUBJECT

Fuel Quantity Indicating System Recalibration

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Boeing 737 MAX 8
Serial Numbers:      MSN 4510 through MSN 5149
Engine(s):           CFM LEAP-1B27
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-BOE-21-588 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address drift in fuel quantity reading observed across multiple operators. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Perform calibration per FCOM 28-41-00, comparing to drip-stick measurement at three fuel levels.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 1,500 flight hours
or 18 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            2 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 4,055
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
