Issue Date:          11/03/2026
Revision Status:     Original Issue
ATA Chapter:         29 — Hydraulic Power
Classification:      Mandatory

SUBJECT

Engine Nacelle Anti-Ice Duct Inspection

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Airbus A330-300
Serial Numbers:      MSN 1948 through MSN 2390
Engine(s):           RR Trent 772B
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-AIR-29-823 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address thermal cycling fatigue cracking observed on bleed air duct elbow. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Inspect duct elbow per Figure 3; replace if any crack length > 6 mm is detected.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 3,000 flight hours
or 6 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            6 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 746
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
