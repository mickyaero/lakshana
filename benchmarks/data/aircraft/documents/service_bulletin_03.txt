SERVICE BULLETIN

Service Bulletin No: SB-DE-27-390
Issue Date:          22/01/2026
Revision Status:     Revision 02
ATA Chapter:         27 — Flight Controls
Classification:      Recommended

SUBJECT

Cargo Door Latch Actuator Software Update

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       De Havilland DHC-6
Serial Numbers:      MSN 1383 through MSN 1673
Engine(s):           PT6A-34
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-DE-27-330 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address rare nuisance unlock indication on EICAS during taxi. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Upgrade actuator firmware from v3.4.1 to v3.5.0 using DCDS loader per maintenance task 52-71-01.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 3,000 flight hours
or 12 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            12 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 2,899
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
