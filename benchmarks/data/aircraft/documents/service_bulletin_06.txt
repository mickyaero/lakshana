SERVICE BULLETIN

Service Bulletin No: SB-AIR-75-560
Issue Date:          28/04/2026
Revision Status:     Revision 01
ATA Chapter:         75 — Engine Air
Classification:      Mandatory

SUBJECT

Cabin Pressure Outflow Valve Position Sensor Update

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Airbus A321neo
Serial Numbers:      MSN 4441 through MSN 5143
Engine(s):           CFM LEAP-1A26
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-AIR-75-884 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address drift of position sensor output observed > 5,000 FH causing slow OFV response. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Replace position sensor P/N OFV-PS-2301 and reprogram CPC per task 21-31-12.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 6,000 flight hours
or 12 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Manhours:            9 MH per aircraft (two technicians, station-level)
Estimated Material Cost: USD 3,259
Special Tooling:     None / Standard line tooling
