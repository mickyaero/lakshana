ATA Chapter:         36 — Pneumatic

SUBJECT

Hydraulic Reservoir Fill Valve Replacement

APPLICABILITY

Aircraft Type:       Boeing 787-8
Serial Numbers:      MSN 3112 through MSN 3841
Engine(s):           GEnx-1B70
Effectivity:         All aircraft within the serial range listed above unless previously
                     modified per SB-BOE-36-443 Rev. 02.

REASON FOR ISSUE

This Service Bulletin is issued to address corrosion of fill valve seat observed in field operations exceeding 8,000 FH. Operator reports and in-service
investigations indicate that continued operation without the corrective action described
herein may result in degraded system performance and increased maintenance burden.

BACKGROUND AND RISK ASSESSMENT

The original design assumption regarding the operating temperature range of this component has been challenged by field experience accumulated over the past 18 months. Investigation of returned units has identified that prolonged exposure to high-cycle vibration in combination with cold-soak conditions can lead to accelerated wear of the internal bearing surfaces. To date there have been 14 confirmed in-service occurrences across the global fleet, none of which have resulted in any safety-of-flight consequence. Engineering risk assessment categorizes the residual risk as Low after compliance with this Service Bulletin.

Residual risk after compliance: NEGLIGIBLE.

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE

Replace fill valve assembly P/N HRV-2241-01 with improved variant P/N HRV-2241-02.

COMPLIANCE

Recommended compliance: Within 4,500 flight hours
or 12 months from the date of this Service Bulletin,
whichever occurs first.

ESTIMATED LABOR

Estimated Material Cost: USD 527
